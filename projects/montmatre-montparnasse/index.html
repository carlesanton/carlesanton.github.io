Interactive application that connected visual art and music to create a multisensory journey through early 20th-century Paris. Developed for the exhibition “From Montmartre to Montparnasse: Catalan Artists in Paris, 1889-1914” at the Picasso Museum, the piece invited visitors to experience the artistic exchanges of that era through both sight and sound.

Audio player displayed at the museum

User interacting with the player

The application played carefully curated audio tracks while displaying contextual information about painters, composers, and their creative intersections. As users explored the content, the app revealed how music and visual art shaped and inspired each other within the vibrant Parisian avant-garde, offering a dialogue between disciplines and senses, evoking the spirit of collaboration and experimentation that defined one of art history's most dynamic periods.
